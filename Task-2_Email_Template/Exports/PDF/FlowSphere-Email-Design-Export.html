About This Project

This document is the design export for CODSOFT UI/UX Internship Task 2 — Responsive Email Template. The email is designed for FlowSphere, a fictional modern SaaS productivity platform.


The email serves as a Welcome & Product Launch communication. It introduces the product, highlights key features, promotes a free trial offer, builds social proof, and drives app downloads.
